About the band

Sukumawikiz band is an afropop band made up of five puppets; Papa, Mama, Kaka, Dada and Mimi.It was created in 2010 by a group of art teachers with the aim of using song and dance to teach small children good values. In 2010, they recorded and released their hit song 'Brush your teeth!'. For the last ten years, they have been travelling all over the country; hosting roadshows and performing in schools.Their work is mostly funded by donations from fans and NGOs.
